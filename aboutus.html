About Us Page

We are a young driven team focused on making a change among young people today in order to shape our future. We strive to make people of the younger and older generations aware of the impacts and consequences surrounding climate change. Our aim is to inspire our users to want to make a change or an impact whether it be something as small as making a donation. Vestigum was engineered for a better and more sustainable future. The world we live in today is very unsustainable for our future and for future generations who will be left to deal with the mess we created. We strongly believe our app has the potential to make a significant change one step at a time. Our team consisted of 5 young, focused undergraduate students who excelled in various aspects of development and production.
